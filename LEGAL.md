# Legal — please read this

**You are responsible for the training data you use.**

gary4local is a tool. It doesn't ship with any LoRA adapters. This repo describes a folder layout — nothing here is itself a trained model.

If you train a LoRA on material you don't own the rights to (a specific artist's catalog, for example), that's on you. Hosting the resulting adapter publicly, or distributing generations that are recognizably derivative of copyrighted work, has its own legal consequences depending on your jurisdiction and use case.

When in doubt: train on your own recordings, public-domain material, or material you're licensed for.

## Intended use

The plugin flow is audio-in → adapter-influenced audio-out, inside a DAW, driven by a human with a prompt and an input take. That's the flow this project is built for.

## Not intended

Taking a LoRA trained on a named artist and using it to mass-generate text2music tracks for upload to streaming services as if you were that artist.

## From the author

If you do train on copyrighted artists — make something truly transformative inside your DAW plz, or just make some good memes. Don't go replacing an artist on Spotify.
